Our Vision for a Connected Future

    Empower doctors to incorporate Ayurvedic knowledge alongside allopathic care for holistic, patient-centered treatment plans.

  • Build the World's Largest Open Repository of Ayurvedic Knowledge

    Digitize and structure classical texts and case studies into an intelligent knowledge graph for research and clinical use.

  • Democratize Access to Ayurvedic Expertise

    Serve as a living bridge between traditional knowledge and modern research, making Ayurveda more relevant and globally respected.

Core AI Features

Intelligent Diagnostic Assistant

Suggests probable doshic imbalances (Vikruti & Prakruti) based on patient inputs. Provides differential diagnosis with confidence levels from Ayurvedic and integrative viewpoints, and supports Nadi & Jihva Pariksha with AI-powered sensor integration.

Personalized Treatment Recommender

Suggests herbal formulations, Panchakarma therapies, and lifestyle changes. Crucially, it warns about contraindications and potential modern drug interactions for integrative treatment plans.

Text & Knowledge Engine

A semantic search interface for classical texts like Charaka Samhita, modern research, and regional knowledge. Features an NLP engine trained on Sanskrit, Malayalam, Tamil, etc., to parse original formulations.

Case-Based Learning & Decision Support

Access curated case repositories with outcomes. A powerful "case similarity" engine compares current patients with past cases for advanced pattern recognition and treatment suggestions.

Herbal Database & Interaction Checker

An extensive Materia Medica with pharmacological actions (Rasa, Guna, Virya, etc.) and modern phytochemistry. Includes a robust interaction checker for polyherbal formulations and allopathic medicines.

Evidence Integration Engine

Links Ayurvedic treatments to available clinical trials from PubMed, AYUSH research portals, and CCRAS. Uses machine learning to predict treatment efficacy based on integrated evidence.

Multilingual & Voice Support

Interface available in English, Sanskrit, Hindi, Malayalam, Tamil, and more. Supports voice-based interaction for faster data input, essential for busy clinics or rural environments.

Patient Management & EHR Integration

A seamless plug-in for existing hospital Electronic Health Records (EHR). Maintains secure patient profiles with Prakruti/Vikruti, treatment history, and progress tracking.

Collaborative Knowledge Graph & Wiki

A community-driven, peer-reviewed wiki for contributing new formulations, folk remedies, and case outcomes. Physicians can contribute and validate entries, building a collective intelligence.

Research Tools for Ayurveda

Provides a suite of tools for designing clinical trials, mining Ayurvedic literature, and standardizing classical formulations for modern pharmacovigilance and validation.

Open Source Principles

  • Modular Architecture
