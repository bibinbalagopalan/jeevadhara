From Fragmented Data to Holistic Insight.

Healthcare data today is trapped in silos—unreadable scans, complex reports, and disconnected patient notes in countless languages. This fragmentation prevents us from seeing the full picture, leading to reactive care instead of proactive prevention.

Jeevadhara (from Jeeva, life + Dhara, flow) is an open-source initiative to unify this chaos. We're building an intelligent stream of health information that empowers doctors, researchers, and public health officials to see earlier, act faster, and understand deeper.

The Power of Unified Intelligence.

A new paradigm in health-tech, built on three core principles.

See the Unseen

Our multi-modal AI reads everything: radiology images (X-rays, CT, MRI), lab reports, and even handwritten notes to identify subtle, early-stage disease markers invisible to the human eye.

Built for India, Ready for the World

Uniquely trained on Indian health data patterns—tackling malaria, TB, and diabetes. Our multi-lingual support and alignment with WHO & ICD standards make Jeevadhara globally adaptable.

Beyond an Individual Diagnosis

Jeevadhara aggregates anonymized data to generate powerful epidemiological insights, mapping disease trends in real-time and enabling predictive alerts for potential outbreaks.

An Open Platform for a Collaborative Future.

Empowering every stakeholder in the healthcare chain.

For Developers & Startups

Access our 100% free repository. Use our powerful APIs & SDKs to integrate Jeevadhara into your applications, join hackathons, and shape the future of health-tech.

For Hospitals & Clinicians

Embed a powerful decision-support tool into your workflow. Get faster, more accurate readings, reduce diagnostic overhead, and focus on what matters: your patients.

For Public Health & NGOs

Leverage real-time health dashboards to monitor disease trends, allocate resources effectively, and deploy preventive measures before outbreaks occur.

Introducing the Digital Health Twin.

Jeevadhara's vision extends beyond diagnostics. By integrating wearable data, voice-based inputs, and lifestyle risk modeling, we aim to create a virtual, long-term health profile for every individual.

  • Predictive alerts based on your unique lifestyle and biometric data.
  • Personalized recommendations for proactive health management.
  • A continuous flow of information for lifelong wellness.
